Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't all of Nova Suite open source?

While we value transparency and community involvement, keeping certain components proprietary allows us to maintain security, ensure consistent quality, and continue providing our services for free.

Can I modify Nova Suite for my needs?

While the core applications cannot be modified, you can create custom integrations using our API and contribute to our open source components.

How are community suggestions implemented?

We regularly review community feedback and incorporate popular and feasible suggestions into our development roadmap.

What's the best way to contribute?

Start by joining our community discussions, reporting issues, or contributing to our open source repositories. Every contribution helps!
